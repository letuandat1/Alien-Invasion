# 🎮 Alien Invasion

## 📌 Overview
**Alien Invasion** is a 2D action shooting game inspired by the *beat ‘em up* and *run and gun* genres.  
The player takes the role of a **Vietnamese soldier** fighting against alien forces to save Earth.

---

## 🌍 Story & Setting
- **Year:** 2055  
- **Setting:** Earth is invaded by alien creatures  
- **Goal:** Eliminate enemies and restore peace  

The player travels through multiple locations, from city environments to enemy bases, uncovering the invasion and fighting back.

---

## 🎮 Core Gameplay
Players control a soldier with the ability to:

- Move and navigate through levels  
- Shoot enemies with different weapons  
- Collect power-ups  
- Survive waves of enemies  

---

## ⚙️ Game Mechanics

### 🔫 Combat System
- Basic shooting  
- Heavy bullets  
- Blaster weapon  

---

### 👾 Enemies
- Eyeworm  
- Acid Worm  
- Space Bat  
- Darkspike (Boss)

Each enemy has different behavior and attack patterns.

---

### 💡 Power-Up System
Power-ups appear:
- At the start of levels  
- When leveling up  

Effects include:
- Increase health  
- Increase damage  
- Increase armor  

---

## 🧍 Characters
- **Main Character:** Vietnamese Soldier  
- **Enemies:** Various alien creatures  

---

## 🗺️ Levels
The game includes 3 main levels:

1. City  
2. Outside enemy base  
3. Inside enemy base
