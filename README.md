## 📌 Overview
Alien Invasion is a **2D pixel action shooting game** where a Vietnamese soldier fights alien invaders to save the Earth.

---

## 🌍 Story & Setting
- Year: 2055  
- Earth is invaded by alien creatures  
- The player must fight enemies and restore peace  

---

## 🎮 Core Gameplay
- Move and explore levels  
- Shoot enemies with different weapons  
- Collect power-ups  
- Survive enemy waves  

---

## ⚙️ Game Mechanics

### 🔫 Combat System
- Basic shooting  
- Heavy bullets  
- Blaster weapon  

### 👾 Enemies
- Eyeworm  
- Acid Worm  
- Space Bat  
- Darkspike (Boss)  

### 💡 Power-Up System
- Increase health  
- Increase damage  
- Increase armor
